Competitive Exams After 12th In Engineering stream

1. Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main

Purpose:

It is a common entrance test conducted to screen candidates into for Admission in B. E./B. Tech., B. Arch., B. Planning in various NITs, IIITs, and other participating institutes. It is also the qualifying exam for JEE Advanced.

Application Mode:

Online

Website:

jeemain.nic.in

2. JEE Advanced

Purpose:

Candidates qualifying in JEE Main can only appear for JEE Advanced which is conducted for admission to UG engineering programs in the prestigious IITs, IISc, ISM Dhanbad and a few other institutes.

Application Mode:

Online mode

Website:

jeeadv.iitd.ac.in

3. BITSAT

Purpose:

BITSAT is conducted to select candidates for admission to engineering and other science programs in BITS Pilani, BITS Goa, and BITS Hyderabad.

Application Mode:

Online

Website:

bitsadmission.com
